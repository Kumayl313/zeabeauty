ZEA BEAUTY — IMAGES
====================

Drop your photos in this folder using these exact filenames. Until you add
them, the site shows soft rose placeholders (it still looks finished).

REQUIRED
--------
hero.jpg     → Reception photo (the curved counter with the ZEA logo on the wall).
                 Wide/landscape works best. ~1600px wide.
room.jpg     → Treatment room with the round backlit mirror.
zeineb.jpg   → Portrait of Zeineb (the photo you attached — her in the beige
                 blazer holding the perfume). Portrait orientation.
logo.png     → Optional: the ZEA BEAUTY logo on transparent background
                 (currently the logo is drawn in text, which matches the design).

GALLERY (square crops, ~800x800)
--------
g1.jpg  → Brows close-up
g2.jpg  → Lashes / eye close-up
g3.jpg  → Salon interior detail (e.g. the ZEA logo shelf)
g4.jpg  → Lips close-up
g5.jpg  → Products + flowers

TIPS
----
- Keep the warm nude / rosy tone consistent across all images — it's what makes
  the site read as "expensive".
- Export as JPG at ~80% quality, or WebP for faster loading.
- After adding a file, just refresh the page. No code changes needed.
